Our Production Process

How we transform your raw concepts into premium finished packaging & print items.

01

Requirement Discussion

We consult with you to understand your product dimensions, material choices (rigid, duplex, corrugated), branding specs, and target quantity.

02

Design Approval

Our prepress team checks your dielines, text traps, bleed sizes, and color profiles (CMYK/Pantone). We proceed only after your digital sign-off.

03

Mass Production

Using advanced high-speed offset or automated digital presses, we begin commercial-grade print runs with absolute color consistency.

04

Quality Check & Delivery

Every single batch undergoes die-cutting, lamination, and structural verification before being securely packed and dispatched to your office.
